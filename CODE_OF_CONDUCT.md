# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be decent. Assume good faith. Argue about ideas, not people.

## Our standards

Behaviour that builds a project worth contributing to:

- Being welcoming to newcomers, whatever their experience level
- Giving and accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the project and its users
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

Behaviour that isn't acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, or personal/political attacks
- Sexualised language or imagery, or unwelcome attention
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Sustained disruption, trolling, or bad-faith argument
- Anything else a reasonable person would recognise as unprofessional

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions — and
when representing the project publicly.

## Enforcement

Report problems to **indugapallignaneswara@gmail.com**. Reports are handled
confidentially. The maintainer may edit or remove contributions that violate
this code, and may ban a contributor temporarily or permanently for behaviour
they consider inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

Enforcement is a judgement call made by a human. If you think a decision was
wrong, say so — respectfully — and it will be reconsidered.
